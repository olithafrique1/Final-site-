About US

 Olith Afrique is a privately held company, independently owned and managed by partners with comprising of highly trained and experienced professional consultants physically in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Cameroon, Benin, and Ivory Coast. The team is comprised of handpicked fine investigators who have been involved in the actual digging of the trenches in the selected countries in Africa. Our consultants comprised of former military, law enforcement, legal and private sector with extensive knowledge operate in most countries in Africa.

We abide by all local laws and regulations with adherence to a strict code of ethics and we emphasize efficiency, innovation and respect for deadlines in everything we do. for more detail.

  Olith Afrique is committed to providing the highest level of investigative services. Brand Protection

Counterfeiting and Product dumping into the local markets both pose great danger to economies. Counterfeiting poses a significant risk to public health and safety... the economic stability and even national security ,while dumping of export products adversely hurts the economy due to duty evasion and companies folding up  as a result of unfair competition. Olith Afrique handles matters related to IP Rights Investigations, IP Rights Enforcement Actions, IP rights Online Monitoring services and IP Rights Custom Protection Services. With the breadth of experience, we have a defined strategy based on professional research and conceptual vision. We thrive to protect our clients against Trademark Infringements, , Design Infringements and Patent Violations.

05

Coordinating Enforcement Actions America.

Olith Afrique team has breadth of experience in coordinating enforcement actions in various countries within Africa. After identifying the producer, importer or distributor of counterfeit products or illicit Products, ... our team relies on its extensive network of Law enforcement agents to coordinate enforcement actions against the infringing party. From presenting the evidence, to detailing the investigative path and findings, to swearing out the complaint on behalf of the client, Olith Afrique will coordinate all aspects of the impending enforcement actions.
